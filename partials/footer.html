When to Go to the Emergency Room

At Sanova Urgent Care, your safety is our priority. If you are experiencing life-threatening symptoms such as severe chest pain, difficulty breathing, major head trauma, or uncontrolled bleeding, please call 911 or visit the nearest emergency room immediately.
