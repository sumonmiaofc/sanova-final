SANOVA — Service Section Images
================================
Add your clinic photos here. Use these exact filenames:

  illness-care.jpg    → Illness & Symptom Treatment section
  injuries-care.jpg   → Injuries & Wound Care section
  xray-labs.jpg       → X-Rays & Lab Testing section
  physicals.jpg       → Physicals & Occupational Health section

Recommended specs:
  Size: 800×600px minimum (4:3 ratio)
  Format: JPG (optimized for web, quality 80-85%)
  File size: Under 300KB each

LOGO:
  Save your logo as: assets/logo.png
  Recommended: PNG with transparent background
  Height: 100px minimum (will be displayed at ~40px height)

HOW IMAGES WORK:
  1. Drop your photo into this folder with the correct filename
  2. The site will automatically show your photo
  3. If no photo is found, a styled placeholder is shown
